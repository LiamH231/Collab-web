    Development

    Created by legendary designer Shingeru Miyamoto, the game was a response to the crash of the video game industry in the early 1980s, aiming to revitalize interest in gaming. The original Super Mario Bros was devloped by five people: director Shigeru Miyamoto, assistant director Takashi Tezuka, programmers Toshihiko Nakago and Kazuaki Morita, and musician Koji Kondo. Super Mario Bros is the first sidescroller to feature Mario, and the game became and international success, selling 40.24 million copies worldwide.

    Reviews

    Most of the Super Mario games have gotten many great reviews and praises from IGN, Gamespot, and Metacritic. Many are calling it the best video game series in the world. The original Super Mario Bros was praised for it's groundbreaking level design and fluid controls. Super Mario 64 revolutionized 3D gaming with it's open world exploration and intuitive controls, and the game earned numerous perfect scores from citics. More recent Super Mario games like Super Mario Odyssey, have been celebrated for their creativity and depth, showcasing Nintendo's commitment to pushing the boundries of game design. The Super Mario Bro's series is loved and recognized by many gamers and critics from around the world.
