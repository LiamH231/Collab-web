About Nintendo

Nintendo was founded in 1889 by Fusajiro Yamauchi as a playing card company, and the company has evolved influential and recognizable names in the video game industry. The company initially produced handmade Hanafuda cards, and overtime the business transitioned into various ventures, including a taxi service and a love hotel. In the 1970s, Nintendo fully embraced the gaming world, and started revelutionizing the gaming world.
